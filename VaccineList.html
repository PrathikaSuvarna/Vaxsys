Moderna-Covid-19


Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. The Moderna vaccine is a vaccine that aims to protect against COVID-19.

Type: RNA

Age: 18-65 years

Number of Doses: 2

Diseases : Coronavirus

Astrazeneca-Covid-19


Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. The Oxford, AstraZeneca vaccine is a vaccine that aims to protect against COVID-19.

Type: Non-Replicating Viral Vector

Age: 18-65 years

Number of Doses: 2

Diseases : Coronavirus

HPV-Human Papillomavirus


Human papillomavirus vaccines are vaccines that prevent infection by certain types of human papillomavirus.

Type: Protein subunit

Age: 9-26/27-45 years

Number of Doses: 2-3

Diseases : Human Papillomavirus

Tetanus


Tetanus Vaccines are vaccines that can help prevent tetanus, an infection caused by Clostridium tetani bacteria.

Type: Toxoid

Age: 2 months-80 years

Number of Doses: 3

Diseases : Tetanus

Hepatitus-A


Hepatitis A vaccine is a vaccine that prevents hepatitis A. It is effective in around 95% of cases and lasts for at least twenty years and possibly a person's entire life.

Type: Inactivated or attenuated

Age: 12 months-65 years

Number of Doses: 2-3

Diseases : Hepatitis

Chicken Pox


Chickenpox is a highly contagious disease caused by the varicella-zoster virus (VZV). Varicella vaccine, also known as chickenpox vaccine, is a vaccine that protects against chickenpox

Type: Attenuated

Age: All

Number of Doses: 1-2

Diseases : Chickenpox

Hepatitus-B


Hepatitis B vaccine is a vaccine that prevents hepatitis B. The first dose is recommended within 24 hours of birth with either two or three more doses given after that.

Type: Subunit

Age: 12 months-65 years

Number of Doses: 2-3

Diseases : Hepatitis

Typhoid


Typhoid vaccines are vaccines that prevent typhoid fever.

Type: Subunit/Oral

Age: 9-45 years

Number of Doses: 1

Diseases : Typhoid

Yellow Fever


Yellow fever vaccine is a vaccine that protects against yellow fever. Yellow fever is a viral infection that occurs in Africa and South America.

Type: Attenuated

Age: 9-12 months

Number of Doses: 1

Diseases : Yellow Fever
